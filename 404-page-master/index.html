My favorite TV show Modern Family and its iconic quotes from the main characters.
Modern Family is a TV show (available on Netflix) about 3 families (who are also related to eachother) and the problems that they face in their relationships! The first family is a very stereotypical one, with three kids (Hayley, Luke and Alex), and parents (Claire and Phil). Claire’s father, Jay is married for a second time to a Columbian woman, who clearly quite a bit younger than him. Her son, Manny, is around 10 years old in the first series, but gets older towards the end. The last family are Jay’s other child, Mitchell and his husband Cameron are a gay couple (however this WILL NOT concern your children, as one myself I am a HUGE supporter of this), who have adopted a baby girl (Lily).
